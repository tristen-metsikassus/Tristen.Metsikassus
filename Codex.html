The vibration of a system in alignment with its core doctrine. High resonance provides stability but creates "Static Heat" that must be discharged through Iselen.

The Fracture

A structural failure in the Harmony Network where doctrine no longer matches reality. If not contained, a Fracture leads to a "Threshold Breach."

Ward-Drift

The natural migration of containment fields over time. Requires constant calibration by the Rowe Institute to prevent slippage.
