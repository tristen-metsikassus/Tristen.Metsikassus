The Architect

The Design Intent

I build worlds that pull people in, challenge what they expect, and leave them thinking long after they close the page. To me, worldbuilding is not just scenery; it is architecture. It is the study of how systems—institutional, magical, and human—interact under pressure.

The World of Rowe is a reflection of this obsession with structural endurance. Every book, every sigil in the archive, and every doctrine in the Codex is a piece of a larger machine designed to explore the cadence of creation and the price of stability.

As a visual worldbuilder and author, my goal is to translate the abstract mechanics of power and resonance into stories that feel as heavy and real as stone. Success is not gaining everything, but understanding the design of what you have built.

— TRISTEN C. METSIKASSUS
