The Bibliography

The Sovereignty Stone

A Prequel to The Rowe Institute Trilogy

Before the Academy was a fortress of doctrine, it was a site of discovery—and dangerous ambition. The Sovereignty Stone traces the origins of the Institute's power and the first harmonic distortions that would eventually lead to the events of the trilogy.

This prequel explores the foundation of the Rowe legacy and the cost of the original "containment" that defined the world Caelum and Aurelia would later inherit.

The Quiet Wing

Book I of The Rowe Institute

At the Rowe Institute, order is everything. Students are measured, disciplined, and shaped to fit a system that prides itself on stability, precision, and control. When Caelum Rook enters that world, he quickly realizes the Academy’s elegance is hiding something older and far more dangerous.

As he is drawn toward the sealed mystery of the Quiet Wing, he begins to uncover a buried history the institution has spent years translating into silence. Meanwhile, Aurelia Rowe, heir to one of the school’s most powerful legacies, is forced into a conflict between the order she was raised to protect and the truth she can no longer ignore.

The Fractured Wing

Book II of The Rowe Institute

After the Harmonic Event, the Rowe Institute does what it does best: it corrects. By morning, the fractured courtyard has been replaced, the official story has already hardened into doctrine, and Caelum Rook has been classified as non-recoverable. But beneath the restored stone, nothing is truly quiet.

The Fractured Wing is the trilogy’s pressure-book — a dark academic novel of institutional dread, moral compromise, and buried continuity — where the system’s visible order begins to crack, and those inside it must decide whether survival is worth becoming part of the machine that is consuming them.

The Warning From Below

Book III of The Rowe Institute

The Rowe Institute was built to contain what it never truly understood. Now, what was buried is beginning to answer. In the aftermath of fracture, failed correction, and the slow collapse of institutional certainty, the survivors are forced beneath the surface of everything they were taught to trust.

The Warning From Below is the final movement of the Rowe Institute trilogy: a dark academic climax of buried truth, institutional reckoning, and ancient contact finally met without illusion.
